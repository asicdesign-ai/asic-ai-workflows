RTL Timing Risk Evaluation Flow

An AI-orchestrated pre-synthesis flow that converts HDL source or textual design views into source-grounded structural timing-risk feedback without claiming STA or signoff timing.

Flowpre-synthesis-timing-risk
Extractorhdl-design-view-extractor

Smoke Casesimple_counter

Executive Summary

The flow performs early timing triage from RTL or AI-readable design views. It identifies structural reg-to-reg timing risks, records evidence, preserves unresolved hierarchy, and emits a final engineering handoff. It is intentionally pre-synthesis: the output is risk and confidence, not slack.

Boundary: the flow can prioritize RTL paths before synthesis, but it cannot replace synthesis, SDC review, gate-level timing, clock-tree analysis, placement, routing, PrimeTime, or Tempus.

Guardrails

  • No real slack or signoff timing claims.
  • No tracing through missing hierarchy or unknown cells as if known.
  • No false-path or multicycle inference without constraints.
  • No claim that synthesis will preserve the same critical path ordering.
  • Model-derived proprietary-language views must carry explicit confidence and unresolved entries.
